Jay and Robyn's child arrangement plan

The children

Jay and Robyn.

Who is agreeing this arrangement

Heather and Richard.

Who they will live with

Jay and Robyn will live with Heather on weekdays.

Who they will spend time with

Jay and Robyn will spend time with Richard.

When visits will happen

Jay and Robyn will see Richard every other weekend starting from 12th December 2025.

What happens during holidays

Heather and Richard agree to make plans with Jay and Robin about what will happen during the school holidays, at least 3 weeks before each holiday.

What happens on special days

Jay and Robyn will stay with Heather for Christmas 2025 and with Richard Christmas 2026 and alternate each year.

How to agree changes

To change scheduled visit times we agree to 48 hours notice. Other changes to be agreed one week prior to the start of a new school term.

Confirm the arrangement

Heather and Richard agree to this arrangement. We agree to review this arrangement every 6 months.
