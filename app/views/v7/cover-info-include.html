Safety check

To make child arrangements you need to be able to state your needs and views without feeling intimidated. Do not continue with this process if you think the other parent is a threat to you or your children's safety.

Do not continue with this process if there has been:

  • any form of domestic abuse or violence, even if the abuse was not directed at the children
  • actual or attempted child abduction
  • child abuse or neglect
  • misuse of drugs, alcohol or other substances
  • any other safety or welfare concerns that place anyone at significant risk of harm

Instead you should find another way to make arrangements by visiting helpwithchildarrangements.service.justice.gov.uk
