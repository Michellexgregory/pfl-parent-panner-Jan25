{{ serviceName }}

This service helps you work out your child arrangements without having to go to court.

You’ll end up with a proposed plan that you can share with another parent or carer to ask their agreement.

Use this service to propose:

  • where your children live
  • when your children spend time with each parent
  • handovers, holidays and whatever else matters to your children

This service is useful if:

  • you are separated or divorced from your children’s other parent and have no child arrangements plan in place yet
  • you want to propose changes to your existing arrangements, even if you have a court order in place
