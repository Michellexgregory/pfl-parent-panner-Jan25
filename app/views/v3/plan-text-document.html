Child arrangement plan for Jay and Robyn

The children

Jay, Robyn

Who is agreeing this plan?

The arrangements

Living and visiting

The children will live with Heather and will be staying overnight with Richard once a week as a flexible arrangement.

What will happen during holidays?

Heather and Richard agree to make plans with Jay and Robin about what will happen during the school holidays, at least 3 weeks before each holiday.

What will happen on special days?

Jay and Robyn will stay with Heather for Christmas 2025 and with Richard Christmas 2026 and alternate each year.

Other things

What other things matter?

We will arrange visits with grandparents and other relatives on an ad-hoc basis.

Decision making

How will you agree changes?

Let's give each other at least 48 hours notice if plans need to change. We should agree changes by text message.
